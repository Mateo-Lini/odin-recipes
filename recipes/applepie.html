Apple pie Recipe

This is a sweet, tart and delicious apple pie.

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup white sugar
  • 2 tbsp flour
  • 1/2 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 tsp nutmeg
  • 1/2 tsp lemon zest
  • 7 cups sliced apples
  • 2 tsp lemon juice
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 1 recipe pastry for a 9 inch double crust pie
